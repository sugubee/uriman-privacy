Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

URIMAN values your privacy. All data is securely stored on your device only. We do not transmit any data to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

URIMAN stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Intimacy Records: Date, time, contraception status, notes
  • Menstrual Cycle Information: Start date, end date
  • Anniversary Information: Dating/marriage start dates, custom anniversaries
  • App Settings: Theme selection, soft expression preferences

2. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored using Hive local database on your device only
  • Data is never transmitted to external servers
  • Data is encrypted within the app
  • No other users or third parties can access your data

3. Advertising

URIMAN uses Google AdMob to display advertisements:

  • AdMob may collect device advertising ID for ad performance measurement
  • Collected information does not identify you personally
  • For more details, see Google Privacy Policy

4. How We Use Information

Collected information is used only for:

  • Recording and displaying intimacy statistics
  • Predicting menstrual cycles
  • Managing anniversaries and notifications
  • Providing personalized experience (themes, expression settings)

5. Information Sharing

URIMAN does not share your personal information with third parties.

Exceptions:

  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • In-app deletion: Use delete button for each record
  • Complete deletion: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data from your device
⚠️ Uninstalling the app permanently and irreversibly deletes all data without backup.

7. Children's Privacy

URIMAN is intended for users aged 19 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 19.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to legal or service changes. We will notify you of changes through in-app notifications.
